    Where does the sound come from when you click an animation in the actor editor?
    Each .ani animation has a sidecar .tani (GATA-format) file in the same folder. The .tani stores tag tracks authored in the editor — including SFX tags (PSS particle paths) and a Sound tag that names a FMOD or Wwise event. The actor editor reads the FMOD path from the Sound tag; at runtime, kg3d_animationtag.dll remaps FMOD paths to their Wwise replacements (DLL string: "AnimationTag wwise replace fmod error: %s"), and the engine plays the resolved Wwise event from the .bnk soundbanks.
